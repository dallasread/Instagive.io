How Does Instagive.io Work?

  1. 1. Three-Minute Setup

    One of the joys of using Instagive.io is the quick, 3-minute sign up process. You'll need your Social Insurance Number and Bank Account information (a blank cheque). Once you have them handy, head over to our sign up page to get started.

  2. 2. Share Your Link

    Instagive.io is social-media friendly. When you sign up, you'll receive a special link that looks like this: instagive.io/your-organization. Share this with your Facebook and Twitter followers to start receiving one-time and recurring donations via credit and debit cards!

  3. 3. Get Paid

    Once you've received a few donations, you'll want to cash in. To withdraw your donations, you need to do absolutely nothing. We hold all donations for 7 days, then deposit them directly into your bank account.
